What is CommIT?

  • Sharing wealth with the People so that they can express their creativity without worrying about money for daily life.
  • Those who nothing contribute to there society, should receive share.
    • But wealth does not need to be shared equally with the People.
    • Sharing percentage should be changed according to each person's contribution.

Why CommIT is needed?

  • The model that Proletariat can receive wealth by helping maintenance of social system, greets the limit.
    • Achievement in information/communication and robotics technology will maintain social system automatically, and take usual human worker's job.

How CommIT is achieved?

  • The internet should be the redistribution system of wealth, by receiving money from Bourgeoisie who wants to notice something to the People to make more money, and sharing it to all the user of internet.
